### §32 告别塑料感：氛围三底层

> 氛围 ≠ Warm tone/ Depressing vibe一键滤镜。必须拆成：**明暗、冷暖、虚实**。

#### 一、控制明暗——用物理光代替情绪形容词

### ❌ 不要


A portrait of a man sitting in a room,
sad atmosphere, depressing dark vibe, gloomy mood, 8k, masterpiece.

- AI 全局压暗 30% → 灰蒙平板，无情绪（对比才产生情绪）

### ✅ 要


A man sitting in near-total darkness, vast black negative space,
a single narrow beam of hard light carving only half his face,
deep chiaroscuro, crushed blacks in background, dramatic contrast ratio

- **大面积绝对阴影** + **吝啬的明确光源** → 不写 sad 观众也能感到孤独

**物理打光公式：**


[大面积暗部] + [单一/极少数光源] + [强对比术语 Chiaroscuro / crushed blacks]


#### 二、控制冷暖——色彩阶级，拒绝端水

### ❌ 不要


warm sunlight, cool blue ambient light, rich colors, atmospheric lighting

- 冷暖 50/50 平均 → 色彩浑浊（Color Pollution），发灰发脏

### ✅ 要


The entire scene is strictly dominated by warm golden-orange sunset light.
Only the shadow edges catch a subtle faint teal-cyan reflection.
Orange-teal cinematic color grading, clean color hierarchy

- **主色调统治全图** + **辅色仅占 ≤10% 角落反光**

**阶级控制公式：**


The entire scene is dominated by [主情绪色/主光].
Only [极小区域] catch a subtle/faint [辅色点缀].


| 情绪 | 主色 | 辅色点缀 |
|------|------|---------|
| 温暖怀旧 | 橙金夕阳 | 阴影边缘微冷青 |
| 寒冷潮湿 | 冷蓝灰雨夜 | 一扇暖窗/路灯 |

#### 三、控制虚实——焦段完成视觉叙事

### ❌ 不要


highly detailed, ultra-detailed background, sharp focus on everything, 8k

- 全屏清晰 = 监控摄像头，无呼吸感；主体像纸片人贴背景

### ✅ 要

**叙事 A：内在孤独（主体实，环境虚）**


Shot on 85mm f/1.4. Subject's eyes razor-sharp in focus.
Background dissolved into soft creamy bokeh light orbs.
Shallow depth of field, subject isolation, intimate loneliness.


**叙事 B：迷失渺小（环境实，主体虚）**


Shot on 35mm. Sharp detailed rainy city street and neon signs.
The lone figure in mid-ground rendered with slight motion blur,
lost in the crowd, deep depth of field on environment.


**焦段叙事公式：**


[镜头/光圈参数] + [主体清晰度] + [背景虚化/动模糊状态]


#### 氛围通用骨架（五区拼接）

### ✅ 要


[媒介与主体] + [虚实控制] + [明暗控制] + [冷暖色彩] + [画质后缀]

示例：
Cinematic medium shot of a woman in a rain-soaked alley, foggy night environment.
Shot on 50mm f/2.0, subject sharp, background melted into bokeh neon orbs.
Crushed blacks in upper frame, a single warm streetlamp key on her cheek, chiaroscuro.
Scene dominated by cold blue-gray; only the lamp casts faint warm amber spill.
Film grain, natural light falloff.
