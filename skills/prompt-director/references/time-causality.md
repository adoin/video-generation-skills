## §11 时间词

**归入文件:** 本文

**原理：** 画面「死」常因只描述三维空间、丢失时间轴。给出时间状态 → AI 启动**时间补偿机制**，自动补因果痕迹。

| 时态 | 补偿逻辑 | 适合情绪 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 过去式 | 根据**结果**反推前一秒痕迹 | 忧郁、怀旧、战损、生活感 |
| 现在进行 | 根据**动势**计算模糊与流变 | 运动、舞蹈、灾难、冲击 |
| 将来时 | 根据**预判**制造势能 | 恐怖、决战前夕、悬念 |

### ❌ 不要

- 只写静态名词：A delicious breakfast on the table→ 广告假亮、无人味
- 只写动作摆拍：A girl dancing ballet→ 罚站感
- 直接画结果：A knight fighting a dragon→ 氛围平、缺张力
- 一个 Prompt 混三种时态 → AI 逻辑混乱
- 用「正在哭」代替「刚哭过」→ 泪水贴图感，缺红肿眼眶等残留

### ✅ 要

**结构：** 主体 + 环境 + 单一明确时间状态 + 风格（每镜只选一种时态）

---

### 过去式｜情绪残留

**关键词：** after, leftover, half-eaten, crumpled, just finished, traces of, worn, disheveled, recently, remnants of

**❌ 不要**


A delicious breakfast on the table.


**✅ 要**


A table after a messy family breakfast,
leftover crumbs, spilt milk, half-eaten toast, napkin crumpled,
morning sunlight striking the dirty plates.


**人像过去式**


A woman who has just finished crying,
swollen eyelids, wet lashes, disheveled hair, subdued atmosphere.


---

### 现在进行｜决定性瞬间

**关键词：** mid-leap, mid-action, billowing, straining, frozen action, high shutter speed, in motion, captured mid-stride, dancing in the light

**❌ 不要**


A girl dancing ballet.


**✅ 要**


A ballet dancer mid-leap, muscles straining,
skirt billowing dynamically, dust motes dancing in the stage light,
frozen action, high shutter speed.


**风场进行时**


A woman walking in wind, coat billowing along wind direction,
hair partially covering face, unforced candid dynamic pose.


---

### 将来时｜势能与张력

**关键词：** moments before, about to, impending, inhaling deeply, extreme tension, scale comparison, on the verge of, poised to

**❌ 不要**


A knight fighting a dragon.


**✅ 要**


A knight standing before a colossal dragon,
moments before the breath of fire, impending doom,
dragon inhaling deeply, throat glowing red,
extreme tension, dramatic scale comparison.


**改写废片：** 在旧 Prompt 加一句 About to [action]或 Just finished [action]，同 Seed 下质感可跃升。

---

### 时态选型速查

| 目标 | 选时态 | 核心写法 |
|------|--------|----------|
| 生活痕迹、怀旧 | 过去式 | 写残留物与凌乱状态 |
| 运动冲击 | 现在进行 | mid-+ 物理应变词 |
| 恐怖/决战 | 将来时 | 写预备动作，不画爆发结果 |

## §13 假因果

**归入文件:** 本文

> 分工：§11 偏时间痕迹；假因果偏虚构原因倒逼质感。与 [prompt-precision.md](prompt-precision.md) §12 幻觉链不同。

**原理：** 虚构一个不必出现在画面里的「因」，倒逼 AI 生成充满细节的「果」——色调、颗粒、微反差自动到位，非 4k masterpiece可替代。

### ❌ 不要

- 只写动作：A girl boxing/ A girl running→ 影楼摆拍，拳套干净、发型整齐
- 只写物理条件：A sunny street→ 宜家样板间，无感染力
- 只写布光术语：rim lighting, dramatic lighting→ 标准肖像，缺戏剧违规感
- 假原因画进画面又写太多 → 抢主体；原因应**暗示**而非堆砌道具

### ✅ 要

**三种假因果技法**

---

### 技法 1｜状态因果（代替动作）

用**状态**暗示动作已持续一段时间 → 汗水、凌乱、疲惫、环境互动自动出现。

**❌ 动作因果（平庸完美）**


A female boxer throwing a punch in the gym, perfect lighting, clean gloves.


**✅ 状态因果（叙事质感）**


A female boxer exhausted after an intense sparring session,
gloves worn and scuffed, hair plastered to face with sweat and oil,
muscles flushed, harsh top light, dark muted background, pyrrhic victory mood.


---

### 技法 2｜情绪因果（情绪即滤镜）

用强烈情绪词代替颜色/光线说明书 → AI 自动调色、加胶片颗粒、调整构图角度。

**❌ 物理描述**


A sunny street with warm light, cozy atmosphere.


**✅ 情绪因果**


An empty childhood bedroom filled with nostalgic silence,
faded wallpaper, dust in sunbeams, desaturated warm tones, film grain,
feeling of someone who lived here but is gone now.


---

### 技法 3｜结果 + 虚构原因（超现实张力）

公式：[极端视觉结果] + [虚构戏剧性原因]

原因可不画进画面，但迫使 AI 打破常规布光。

**❌ 正向布光**


Portrait of a man, rim lighting, dramatic lighting, dark background.


**✅ 结果后置**


A face illuminated by the holy glow of a cybernetic angel descending,
high contrast, divine interference causing glitch artifacts in the air.


---

### 课后模板：雨中等人


❌ A person waiting in the rain, sad, cinematic
✅ A person who has been waiting in the rain for a long time,
   soaked coat, tired eyes with catchlight, rain streaks on blurred neon background,
   lonely but dignified, not homeless poverty aesthetic
